Gratz

About

Gratz is a self-produced multi-instrumentalist, vocalist, songwriter, and artist. His music is centered around the darker side of emotions, inspired by RnB, Grunge, Soul, Funk, and Dance Music. Gratz cultivates his influences to try and portray the sound he hears in his head and share it with the world.
